Challenges With Signed Languages

Human language are complex and diverse.We express ourselves in many ways, such through speaking or signing.unlike spoken language, sign language are visual. user need to process that visual elments to understand sign language. that may cause challanges with it.

Deaf culture and community

Major Component Of Sign Language

Each sign is made up, or parameters, performed by the signer to convey its meaning. I.S.L.R has developed a method for detecting these important feature in order to fully recognize sign language.

HANDS

The shape, orientation, movement, and location of the hands are some of the key parameters of sign language.

Capturing And Digitizing The Elements Of Sign Language

I.S.L.R's corer technology uses visual input from user and convert this information into data which process by computer.

Visual input can be collect from one camera or multiple camera.

The Process of converting Data Into Meaningfull Information

Capture The Visual

The camera or webcams capture the images or frames as a visual input.

Process The Data

Image represent sign which may have multiple meaning, similar forms. nural network process images and retrive posssible sign.

Narrow the selection

In some cases, there could be over 100 combinations of the sign, as they can also represent different syntactic roles within the sentences. Using a variety of machine translation approaches, the probability of different combinations is calculated, and the most likely options are identified.

Predict Translation

The most likely results are displayed on the screen for the user. The user then read massege properly.

Present the output

The sentence is displayed on the screen for the hearing user to read. It can also be voiced using text-to-speech technology.
